03 / CAPSTONE — FINAL YEAR PROJECT · workin/release-v1 · skip ahead anytime

WORKIN

An on-demand worker marketplace. Verified local labor, on a live map, in real time.

01 — THE PROBLEM

Finding a verified electrician in a city of four million runs on phone calls and luck.

Hiring skilled local labor in Pakistan is word-of-mouth: no identity checks, no live status, no idea when — or if — anyone shows up. WorkIn replaces the phonebook with a radius. Open the map, and verified workers appear around you as the search pulse expands.

02 — THE BUILD

Maps, sockets and video calls — one Flutter codebase.

  • ▸ Live worker tracking on a night map — Google Maps Platform + Routes API
  • ▸ An expanding search-radius pulse that recruits workers as it grows
  • ▸ Identity verification through an in-app camera flow → Cloudinary
  • ▸ In-app voice & video between customer and worker — Agora RTC
  • ▸ Firestore + FCM keep job status honest; MVVM + Provider keep the code honest
